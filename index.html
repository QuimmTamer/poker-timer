📱 Installing on iPhone (No App Store Needed)
  1. Open this page in Safari — it must be Safari, not Chrome or Firefox
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari
  3. Scroll down the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Give it a name (e.g. "Poker Timer") and tap "Add"
  5. The app icon now appears on your Home Screen and opens fullscreen — no browser chrome

Note: The app works offline after the first load. All your settings are saved to your device.
📺 Casting to a TV
  1. Make sure your iPhone and Apple TV / AirPlay TV are on the same Wi-Fi network
  2. Open the Poker Timer app from your Home Screen (fullscreen looks best)
  3. Swipe down from the top-right corner to open Control Center
  4. Tap Screen Mirroring and choose your TV
  5. Navigate to the Timer tab — the large display is optimized for TV viewing

Pro tip: Enable Do Not Disturb and keep your iPhone plugged in — screen mirroring drains battery fast.
⏱️ Using the Timer
  • Tap the gold circle button to start or pause the countdown
  • Use ◀ Prev and Next ▶ to manually move between levels
  • The timer auto-advances and plays a chime when a level ends
  • The timer turns orange with 60 seconds left as a visual warning
  • During a break, the timer turns blue and shows break time remaining
  • Tap the speaker icon to mute/unmute all sounds
👥 Player Registration
  • Use +/− at the top to add or remove player seats
  • Tap a player name to rename them
  • Tap Buy In to mark a player as entered — this adds to the prize pool
  • Use the Rebuy +/− to track additional buy-ins during the tournament
  • Tap ✕ to mark a player as eliminated — they appear dimmed
  • The prize pool and payout splits update live as players enter and rebuy
⚙️ Adjusting Settings
  • Set the Tournament Name — it displays prominently on the timer screen
  • Set Buy-In, Rebuy, and Add-On amounts for automatic prize pool math
  • Set Level Duration for how long each blind level runs
  • Set Break Every N Levels (e.g. 4 = break after levels 4, 8, 12...)
  • Set Rebuy Cutoff Level — players can't rebuy after this level number
  • Toggle Ante on/off — uses the ante column from the blind structure
  • Edit the Blind Structure to customize any level's small blind, big blind, and ante
  • Set Payout percentages — they must add up to 100%
  • Always press Save Settings — settings persist across sessions
💡 Tips
  • Keep the app open on screen while hosting — minimizing too long may pause the timer on older iPhones
  • The timer screen is designed to be readable from across the room on a TV
  • Prize pool updates instantly as players buy in — great for transparency
  • Rebuy cutoff level is shown live on the timer screen so players always know
  • Run the app fullscreen from your Home Screen for the cleanest TV cast
